Tikka-Boti-Recipe

ingredient Required

  • 1-kg Meat thin small cubes
  • Garlic Paste 2-3tablesspoon
  • oil /butter 2 cup, melted for basting
  • shan Tkka masala

    • Tender Steak

  • Steak 2 kg
  • cooking oil 2, cups
  • shan Tikka masala 1, paket

STEPS OF COOKING

Tikka Boti

  1. mix SHAN TIKKA MASALAin half cup of water ,Garlic and 2, tablespoons oil.Apply to meat.
  2. Leave to marinate for 3-4 hours . * (Remove and discard the raw papaya slices from the meat ,if used).
  3. Sew meat onto skewers. Place on very low heat of coal/gas fire. Grill lightly on all sides.
  4. lightly brush meat with butter /oil and roatate the skewers until meat is tender. Serve with SHAN tAMARIND cHUTMEY, SLALD and lemon juice. *to tenderize the meat further , add 1 cup thinly sliced papaya.

Tender Steak

  1. mix all the above and marinate for 1-2 houts.
  2. Heat a pan and place the steak for 3 minutes on the one side and 1-2 minutes on the other side .
  3. Do not press the meat . Remove and let it cool down for 1-2 minutes before serving
